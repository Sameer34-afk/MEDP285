What is Chrome?

Chrome is often confued as a color but could rather be a considered a material or a type of finish. It has a metallic nature to it and is known for it's shiny, reflective type surface.

Why is Chrome Popular?

Chrome has been increasingly popular with the rise of the Y2K era. It signified values in industrial design,modernity and sleekness. In it's essence chrome has a sense of futiristic design and gives people a sense of "coolness" when they see it.
